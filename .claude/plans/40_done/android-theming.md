## Desired Outcome
Material 3 dynamic theming with 6 theme options matching iOS: Monet (light), Turner (light), Malevich (dark), Bauder (dark), Klimt (dark), Majorelle (dark). DS tokens for spacing, radius, text sizes, icon sizes, opacity, duration. Theme picker in settings.

**Files (iOS reference):** Theme.swift, Theme+Palettes.swift, Colors.swift
