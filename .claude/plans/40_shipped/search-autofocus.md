# Search bar auto-focus
## What changed
Opening the conversation search sheet now auto-focuses the search bar and brings up the keyboard.

## How to test
1. Tap the search button to open the search sheet
2. Keyboard should appear immediately without needing to tap the search field

## Expected result
Search field is focused and keyboard is visible as soon as the sheet opens.
