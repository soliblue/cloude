# Android Whiteboard


## Desired Outcome
Canvas with freehand drawing, rectangles, circles, arrows, text elements. Sketchy hand-drawn style rendering. Touch gestures (pan, zoom, select). Export to image. This is the most complex feature and lowest priority for parity.

**Files (iOS reference):** WhiteboardSheet.swift (+11 extension files), WhiteboardStore.swift (+6 extension files), WhiteboardElement.swift
