# Artist Themes {paintpalette.fill}
Four new dark themes inspired by artists/architects, each occupying a unique color territory.

## Themes
- **Gaudí** - forest green (Park Güell ceramics). Icon: `building.columns.fill`
- **Hundertwasser** - warm terracotta/clay. Icon: `house.fill`
- **Rothko** - deep wine/burgundy. Icon: `square.fill`
- **Klimt** - amber gold (The Kiss). Icon: `sparkles`

## Changes
- `Theme.swift`: Added 4 new cases with palettes and icons
