## Feb 15 — Travel to Marrakech (10:30pm)
### Airport → Earth Hostel
- 15-20 min taxi, 5km
- Petit taxi from rank outside arrivals (beige cars)
- Agree price BEFORE getting in — 70-100 MAD (€7-10). They will try 200-300.
- Say confidently: "Medina, 100 dirham." Walk to next taxi if they refuse.
- Have exact cash (they claim no change)
- Taxi stops at nearest medina gate — short walk to hostel
- Alternative: ask hostel about pickup (+212 6510-542-81)

## Feb 16 — Medina Day
### 🌞 Morning coffee + Ben Youssef Madrasa
HeiBai Coffee — minimalist specialty café, precision brewing, floor-to-ceiling windows. Or The Bloom Room for iced coffees, matcha, cinnamon rolls.

Ben Youssef Madrasa (50 MAD/pp) — intricate Islamic architecture, one of North Africa's largest.

### 🕛 Le Jardin Secret → Souks for lunch
Le Jardin Secret (80 MAD/pp) — peaceful hidden garden, two restored Islamic gardens. Good midday escape from medina heat.

Walk through souks → eat near Jemaa el-Fnaa when hungry.

### 🕝 Bahia Palace → Saadian Tombs
Bahia Palace (70 MAD/pp) — opulent 19th century palace with stunning courtyard gardens → Saadian Tombs (70 MAD/pp) — 5 min from Bahia, beautiful tilework from the 1500s.

### 🌑 Sunset drinks → Jemaa el-Fnaa → optional hammam
Sunset drinks — El Fenn rooftop (~150 MAD) or DarDar (~120 MAD) → Jemaa el-Fnaa at night (peaks 8-10pm) — food stalls, storytellers, snake charmers, performers.

Optional: Hammam Mouassine — 16th century, oldest operating hammam in Marrakech. Three temperature chambers, black soap scrub. ~100 MAD entrance.

### Live Music Options
- Churchill Bar (La Mamounia) — velvet, red leather, live jazz/blues, smart dress
- Le Comptoir Darna — live bands, upscale dining + belly dancing
- Café Clock — Moroccan folklore and traditional music

## Feb 18 — Travel to Essaouira
### 🌞 Maison de la Photographie → Mellah
Maison de la Photographie (opens 9:30am) — rooftop mint tea with Atlas views. Then wander down to the Mellah and Lazama Synagogue. Relaxed 2-3 hours, leaves plenty of time for a last lunch in the souks.

### Transport
- Last lunch in souks, then head to bus station
- Supratours bus — take 2:45pm or 4:45pm departure
- Duration: 3h | Cost: 140 MAD (~€13)
- Book: supratoursbus.com, 12go.asia, bookaway.com, or buy at station (cheaper)
- Arrive 30min early
- ⚠️ Avoid arriving during iftar (6-7:30pm) — everything shuts down

### 🌑 Evening in Essaouira
Walk the medina at night. Post-iftar the streets come alive — open restaurants after 7:30pm. Taros rooftop is a good first-night spot — cocktails, music, transforms into dance floor by 9pm.

## Survival Tips
### Ramadan (Feb 17 – Mar 19)
- Most local restaurants close daytime or limited hours (10am-4pm)
- Tourist hotels, riads, international chains stay open
- Don't eat/drink/smoke publicly during daylight — respect, not law
- Eat in your accommodation during the day
- Alcohol harder to find — high-end hotels serve indoors with food
- ⚠️ Avoid traveling 6-7:30pm — be at destination by 5:30pm (iftar shutdown)
- Streets quieter during day, VERY lively after sunset
- Dinner service starts later (8pm+) to align with iftar

### Scam Awareness
- "Helpful" strangers = fake guides. Say "La, shukran" and keep walking
- If lost, go INTO a shop and ask owner (won't leave their store)
- Photos at Jemaa el-Fnaa = they WILL demand 20-50 MAD
- Taxi change scam — have exact cash
- Someone says "it's closed" = ignore, verify yourself

### Money
- ATMs everywhere (Visa/Mastercard), dispense 100/200 MAD — break them immediately
- Keep 500-1000 MAD in small bills (20s, 50s) daily
- Cash > cards for bargaining power
- 1 EUR ≈ 11 MAD

### Bargaining
- Seller quotes price → quarter it (they'll laugh = game starts)
- Final price = 1/3 to 1/2 of initial quote
- Shop early morning = first sale is "good luck", better deals
- Use Arabic! "ghali ktir" (too expensive), "ana masri" (I'm Egyptian)
- Be polite, friendly — it's a sport, not a battle

### Food & Safety
- Busy street food stalls = safe (high turnover), empty stalls = avoid
- Bottled water only, no tap water
- Covered shoulders/knees in religious areas
- Medina at night = safe in main areas, avoid dark alleys alone
- Sunset ~6:30pm — plan rooftop drinks for 6pm

### Shopping
Real argan oil = pharmacies (80-120 MAD), NOT souks (often fake).

### Medical
Pharmacies everywhere, sell antibiotics OTC. If severe diarrhea: Imodium + ORS packets first, buy Azithromycin at pharmacy if needed.

### Quick Ref
- 1 EUR ≈ 11 MAD
- Street food: 20-40 MAD (€2-4)
- Restaurant: 80-150 MAD (€7-14)
- Rooftop cocktail: 120-170 MAD (€11-15)
